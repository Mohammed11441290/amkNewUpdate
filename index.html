Who We Are?

AMK Consulting Engineers Limited is a new structural & civil design consultancy, established in 2013.

Providing consultancy services for domestic extensions, refurbishment, new build & commercial property. We also specialise in building inspections for solar PV installations, working in conjunction with Solmatix Renewables Ltd, timber frame design, steel work connections to BS5950 / BS EN 1993, aluminium curtain walling design, temporary works design, value engineering & 3D visualisations. We can also provide assistance with planning applications, site analysis, flood risk assessments, traffic impact assessments and road junction design.read more →
